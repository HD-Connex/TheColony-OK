The Host

JAKE MERRICK · CANDIDATE FOR GOVERNOR

Jake Merrick

Jake Merrick is a small business owner, lifelong Oklahoman, and candidate for governor. He launched The Colony Report in 2026 to do the kind of long-form political analysis legacy press won't.

Three nights a week, he sits down with a single guest — a legislator, a journalist, a constituent — and works through the story for an hour. No commercials. No talking points.
