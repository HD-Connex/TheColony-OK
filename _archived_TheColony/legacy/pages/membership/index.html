FAQ

CANCELATION · ACCESS · BILLING
What's included in membership?
Everything we publish: investigative articles, full podcast library across all four shows, live broadcasts and replays, members-only weekday briefings, and bonus podcast episodes in your members-only feed.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Sign into your account and click "Cancel Subscription." No contracts, no penalties, no awkward retention calls. Your access continues to the end of the current billing period.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes — full refund within the first 14 days, no questions asked. After that, you can cancel any time and avoid future billing.
How do I sign in?
Enter your email on the Account page. We send a one-time login link — no passwords to remember.
Is my payment secure?
Yes. Checkout runs on Stripe's hosted infrastructure. We never see or store your card details.
Where does the money go?
Reporting. Roughly 70% to journalist salaries and freelance investigative work, the rest to production, hosting, and the small team that keeps the lights on. We publish an annual transparency report.
Are there annual or family plans?
Not yet — the founding cohort is monthly so we can iterate. Annual and family plans roll out in Q3.
